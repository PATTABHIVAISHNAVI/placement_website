GRE EXAM

GRE is Graduate Record Exam which is conducted every year which is conducted by ETS (Educational Testing Service). This test can be attempted in both the ways i.e, in computer based and paper based so we have both online and offline facilities. This exam is mainly written by the students who are willing to study in abroad in thier dream university. Once the Exam is written it is valid upto 5years.It has 2 sections apptitude and the subject section. These sections contains Verbal Reasoning, Quantitative Reasoning, and Analytical Writing section. There are no sections for the GRE subject test.

UPSC EXAM

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) releases exam notices of the various recruitment examinations every year. There is a vast array of services for which UPSC Exams are held. The Civil Services Examination recruits candidates for Group A and Group B services and posts Every year the upsc examination is conducted. Lakhs of students apply for this examination but only hundreds of them are selected. This exam is conducted in three stages.

  1. Preliminary stage
  2. Mains exam stage
  3. Interview stage
      4. There is an age limit to write the examination. This exam can only be written by the people who are between 21years to 32years. Only then they are eligible to write the examination.This exam rest you on the Apptitude test, General studies,Environment,Languages and it even has one optional subject where we have a choice to choose that optional according to our wish. This exam can be written in 2 languages only i.e, Hindi and English. Only by writing one exam we have option to get many posts like IAS,IFS,IPS,IRS etc., based on our rank and our category.In order to write this examination we need to have a graduation degree in our hand.
